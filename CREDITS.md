# Credits & Attribution

The pixel character art used and produced by **LPC Pattern Clothier** comes from the
[Universal LPC Spritesheet Character Generator](https://github.com/liberatedpixelcup/Universal-LPC-Spritesheet-Character-Generator),
part of the [Liberated Pixel Cup](https://lpc.opengameart.org) (LPC) project. Heartfelt thanks to every
artist and maintainer who created and curated this art and made it freely available. 🙏

This tool merely re-skins their work; **all character artwork belongs to the LPC artists.**

## You must credit the authors

Every spritesheet produced by this tool is a derivative of LPC art. If you use it in a project you must
credit the original authors (everything except CC0 assets) and follow the licence(s) of the assets you used.

The **authoritative, per-asset** list of authors, licences and source URLs is maintained upstream:

- **CREDITS.csv:** <https://github.com/liberatedpixelcup/Universal-LPC-Spritesheet-Character-Generator/blob/master/CREDITS.csv>

Distribute that file (or a composed subset covering just the assets you used) with your project, and make it
reasonably discoverable to your users (e.g. on a credits screen or via a visible link).

## Supported licences (per asset)

LPC art is distributed under one or more of:

- **CC0** — public domain; attribution not required.
- **CC-BY 4.0** — must credit the authors.
- **CC-BY-SA 3.0 / 4.0** — must credit the authors **and** release derivative artwork under CC-BY-SA. (This is
  the most restrictive licence commonly present; the no-DRM clause can matter for some storefronts.)
- **OGA-BY 3.0** — must credit the authors (DRM allowed).
- **GPL 3.0** — must distribute derivative artwork/modifications under GPL.

It is your responsibility to comply with the specific licence(s) of the assets you select.
